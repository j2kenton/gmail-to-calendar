EventGrabber

EventGrabber for Gmail™ and Google Calendar™ makes it effortless to turn emails into calendar events using the power of Gemini AI.

Open any email containing a meeting, appointment, or event. Click Send to Calendar, and Gemini AI extracts the title, date, time, and location, and creates the calendar event for you instantly.

Features

  • Works with English and Hebrew emails
  • Understands both absolute dates ("October 15th at 3:00 PM") and relative dates ("tomorrow", "next Tuesday")
  • Uses your own Gemini API key — no data stored by the developer
  • Free to use (requires a free Gemini API key from Google AI Studio)

Getting started

  1. Install the add-on
  2. Open any email in Gmail™ — the sidebar will appear automatically
  3. Click the settings icon and enter your free Gemini API key (available at aistudio.google.com)
  4. Open any email with an event and click "Send to Calendar"
  5. Check your Google Calendar™ — your event is ready
